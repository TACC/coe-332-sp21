Week 10: Container Orchestration with Kubernetes, continued
===========================================================

In this week, we complete our treatment of the Kubernetes orchestration system by introducing two new resource types:
Deployments and Services. We will be using deployments to describe our applications and we will use services to
expose our applications to the network.
